DLF Limited (Delhi Land & Finance)[2]) is the largest commercial real estate developers in India and the owner is Kushal Pal Singh. It was founded by Chaudhary Raghvendra Singh[3] in 1946 and is New Delhi, India based .[4] DLF developed residential colonies in Delhi such as Shivaji Park (their first development), Rajouri Garden, Krishna Nagar, South Extension, Greater Kailash, Kailash Colony, and Hauz Khas. DLF builds residential, office, and retail properties.
